🍟 TABITACOS

Fast Food Kunuhay — Cebu's Home of Crispy, Saucy, and Affordable Eats

🏢 About the Company

Tabitacos is a Filipino fast food brand founded in Cebu City, dedicated to serving hot, fresh, and budget-friendly meals to every Juan. From our signature fries to hearty burgers, every bite is made with love and the freshest local ingredients.

🎯 Our Mission

To make great-tasting, quality fast food accessible to every Filipino — whether you're grabbing a quick lunch, celebrating with family, or just craving something good after a long day.

👁️ Our Vision

To be Cebu's most-loved homegrown fast food brand — growing from a single stall to a household name, one serving of fries at a time.

💛 Core Values

  • Quality Ingredients
  • Honest Pricing
  • Fast & Friendly Service
  • Community First
  • Hygiene & Food Safety
